Welcome, International Guests!

Permanent Fever is a traditional Japanese "snack bar" — a cozy karaoke lounge run by a warm hostess called Mama. Think of it as a neighborhood bar with karaoke, great conversation, and delicious food in a fun retro atmosphere.

Located in Gion, Kyoto — perfect as your second stop of the night, or your first authentic snack bar experience. English menus available, and basic English conversation is possible.
